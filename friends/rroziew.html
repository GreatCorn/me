GreatCorn met Rroziew through Misha, they played Roblox together, with Ragy joining the group later.

Rroziew was one of the first to start the Kurlykistan lore and he was the one who gave GreatCorn the nickname Vorobushek Blin Krevetovich. Many of Ragy's inside jokes about muslims or close in resemblance involved Rroziew.

Rroziew's character is mostly chill, though he is most famous for his high-pitched laugh, heard first in ДВЕ МИНУТКИ РОБЛОКСА (TWO MINUTES OF ROBLOX), which marks his first appearance on GreatCorn's channel.
