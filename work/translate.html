Ъ Trъnslъtъ

FORM: Webpage, Chrome App

ADDRESS: greatcorn.github.io/translate/

LATEST UPDATE: 30.06.2020

CREATION DATE: 23.02.2020

DESCRIPTION: A "translator" to many of the Kurlykistanian dialects. Includes Ъ (clean, Vialeny Pirog), Ь (clean, Tvorozhnoje Miaso), ÖЪ (Blyat), Å (Schwedishe), ЪЬ (Zarena Kaczelja), ÖЬ (Blyachi Pruzhinski), Khbvrylîss (Kurlykongard current), Курлыкистaнскїй (Kurlykistanian), Polskie (Polish), Српски (Serbian), 日本人 (Japanese) and custom.

DISCLAIMER: The Polish, Serbian and Japanese translators aren't real. They are implemented just for entertainment purposes.

This was the first public HTML work by me and my first GitHub Pages website. The meaning behind the Ъ is that when replacing all vowels with it in a sentence and then running that sentence through Google Translate (or maybe another translator) on Bulgarian, the result may be humorous and irrelevant to the original text. As far as I know, in Bulgarian, Ъ acts as a vowel.

The translator can also be installed as a Chrome app.
