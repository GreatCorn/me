DREATH

FORM: Videogame

GENRE: Horror

DURATION: About a half-hour

DATE: 06.09.2020

DESCRIPTION: DREATH is an experimental horror game based on a dream. The final and major objective is to wake up by recalling certain memories. The progression of the game relies on how the player perceives the situation it is posing.

The player sees himself in the eyes of an unknown man, who is lost in his dream. The purposely forgotten and rejected memories come back, deformed and misshapen and it is up to the player to decipher the problem and make the final choice.
