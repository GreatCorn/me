Symphony №1

FORM: Single, Sheet Music (WIP)

GENRE: Symphony

DURATION: 27:08

DATE: 19.06.2021

DESCRIPTION: This symphony was actually quite accidental. I thought it would be better to leave big works like symphonies for when I'd be a little older. I wrote the second movement (rondo) without thinking about it being in a symphony. Then wrote another orchestral piece (first movement) and realized the rondo would fit it. I was sometimes thinking about WW2 and the tragic subject of war in general when composing it, especially the fourth movement.

CONTENTS:

  • I. Allegro deciso (05:19)
  • II. Moderato comodo (07:36)
  • III. Scherzo. Allegretto con moto (04:29)
  • IV. Finale. Con moto e marziale (09:39)
