MASMZE-3D

FORM: Videogame

GENRE: Horror

DURATION: A few minutes

DATE: 02.07.2023

DESCRIPTION: MASMZE-3D is made almost entirely on x86 Assembly and native WinAPI procedures with the help of the Microsoft Macro Assembler (MASM32).

The objective, for now, is to get through the maze layer by layer and delve deeper into it. The game is still being worked on and I plan to add more variety.
