Kurlykistan is pretty much a fictional world a former nation, now an undercover organization, first being named the Cyrian Regiments, later Kurlykongard, and finally, Kurlykistan in the lore. It was founded by GreatCorn with the help of his friends. The beginnings were split up between videos and time. The first call was on a video from 13.07.2016, Кряканье Bandicam (Cracking Bandicam), where GreatCorn called himself a shrimp. This was due to a song by the band Los Estramboticos, La Cerveza y El Dolor being stuck in his head and the lyrics "mas cerveza", being close in sound to "я креветка" (I'm a shrimp). The second call was in the famous МИНУТКА РОБЛОКСА, where GreatCorn called himself a pancake repeatedly by accident ("я, блин", in which "блин" could be translated as both "pancake" and "damn"). The last one was on a Roblox stream with Misha, Rroziew and Ragy, where the guys were playing Before the Dawn in Roblox and due to sitting on a branch, GreatCorn repeatedly said that he's a sparrow. After these events, his friends (most notable, Rroziew) began calling him Vorobushek Blin Krevetovich (literally Sparrow Pancake Shrimp). This happened at the end of 2017.

The new sequel to МИНУТКА РОБЛОКСА, ДВЕ МИНУТКИ РОБЛОКСА, featured the Vorobushek Blin Krevetovich concept in the thumbnail. The symbol, uniting a sparrow, a pancake, a shrimp, and an ear of corn on the cob was later used as the symbol of the Cyrian religion, Åotir Mundý. GreatCorn later makes the Kurlykistanian flag, the Kurlykistan Skype group, and profile pictures for his friends.

After some time, there is a Viber group created and GreatCorn begins to label his work with Kurlykistanian Alliance. He starts posting pictures of some Kurlykistanian events. Great Tomato forms his own world with the Tomatoland airship. Later GreatCorn writes a short story of how Kurlykistan was a secret alliance that helped the Third Reich in World War 2, later to form as an independent nation, but fll after the Great Kurlykistanian Battle with Tomatoland, the loss of the Third Reich in the war and the arrival of the Red Army. He writes about Mivina, a common in Ukraine 5-minute-foods and spices brand, which is actually a plant that has strong healing powers. LIGHTNINGMIVINA and his brother help introduce the Kurlykistanian currency, kurlyk (курлык), ⓚ. GreatCorn tries to incorporate everything into a fiction novel, but fails.

At the end of March 2019 Basya temporarily took rule over Kurlykistan after GreatCorn's short disappearance. Kurlykistanian presidential elections took place in which participated: GreatCorn, Basya, Misha, Great Tomato and Rroziew. In the end, GreatCorn got most of the votes and went as the president of Kurlykistan. Many inside jokes get incorporated into the lore and then, in 2020, GreatCorn decides to start writing a proper history book about Kurlykistan.
